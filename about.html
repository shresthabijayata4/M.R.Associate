About

M.R. Associates [MRA] is a firm registered under The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal [ICAN] in 2073 with firm registration number 640. The registered office of MRA is at Kathmandu-31, New Baneshwor. MRA is a growing firm with capability to deliver audit and related financial services to a wide range of businesses in Nepal. We strive to create high value for money to our valued clients. Integrity and professional behavior are our key features which we reflect in all our assignments. We have the experience and professional resources to provide varied business services on diverse sectors like Development Sector, Hospitality Sector, Banking and Financial Sector, Hydroelectricity, Trading and Manufacturing Sector, including SMEs and MEs. We strive to be one of the leading Chartered Accountants firm in the years to come.

Our Vision

We strive to be the most trusted professional service provider over multi-dimensional sectors in Nepal.
